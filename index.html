Program Overview

Preparing high school seniors for college success in healthcare fields

The Growing Leaders Pre-College Program, initiated by Alpha Phi Alpha's Gamma Lambda Chapter in collaboration with Michigan State University and Henry Ford Health, is designed to prepare high school seniors for collegeâ€”particularly in healthcare fields. Our mission: increase college enrollment rates by providing hands-on mentorship, academic readiness, and real-world professional experiences.

Program Goals

Academic Excellence

Build college & career readiness skills and improve time management and study habits

Career Exploration

Explore career and major options in health-related fields

Leadership Development

Develop networking and leadership abilities for lifelong success

Personal Growth

Increase self-confidence and motivation through guided support

Who Can Participate?

Eligible Students

High school seniors who want to:

  • Strengthen academic skills
  • Learn about college life firsthand
  • Gain access to mentors and professional resources
  • Northwestern and University Prep students

Scholarship Opportunity

Northwestern and University Prep students attending MSU are eligible for scholarships.
